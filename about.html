About

Hi, thanks for viewing my site. I presume that by viewing the site you require the services of a photographer. Hopefully I can assist your requirements. I come from a Fine Art background having studied photography at Stourbridge College of Art, now in the distant past. Apart from teaching photography at 3rd level I have been in the profession ever since. I have worked on location and in studios using all varieties of equipment from 5x4 film plate cameras to the latest digital devices. But talking about the type of cameras etc I use is not really an important issue for me as I believe that it’s how you envisage an image that is fundamental to good photography. After all a cardboard box with a small hole in it, a pinhole camera, can take perfectly good photographs if that is what the image requires.

This website is a showcase exclusively for my property, food, and portraiture portfolio. You can also view my personal work at www.pboylephotos.com (currently under construction). I have gained the experience necessary to provide clients with a professional and skilled body of images. I believe I am open and approachable and value straightforward easy discussions with clients about their requirements in the marketplace. Please feel free to contact me if you need more information. Photography is a visual medium so hopefully the images speak for themselves
